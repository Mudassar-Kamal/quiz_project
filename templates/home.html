Quiz Rounds

QUIZ competition consists of two rounds — Elimination Round and Grand Finale, both rounds are designed to take about 20 minutes to complete. Any student from your school can participate in this exciting English & Science Competition. Detail is given below.

Elimination Round
  • All registered Students will participate in the ‘Elimination Round’.
  • There will be 20 questions (MCQs) to be attempted in 20 minutes.
  • Contestants, scoring below 90% will be eliminated & above will qualify for ‘Grand Finale'.
  • Marks scored in the Elimination Round of the quiz will not be carried forward to the Grand Finale of the quiz.
Grand Finale
  • The winners of the Elimination round will participate in the ‘Grand Finale’ of the Quiz.
  • Grand Finale will be held online and the winners will be selected on the basis of Score and Time.
  • There will be 30 questions (MCQs) to be attempted in 20 minutes. Marks of previous round will not be carried forward to the Grand finale.
